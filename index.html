Kinetic Canvas

  • Kinetic Canvas is an app to play with art.
  • Use your finger to paint stunning animated patterns that overlap and form new shapes and colours.
  • There are thousands of random combinations, so every time you play is unique.
  • This is not a game - there are no levels, no score, no challenges - it’s just you, creating light and colour.

Features

  • Easy to play - Draw by tapping or clicking.
  • Beautiful visuals - Create art with soft colours and smooth animations.
  • Always different - Every time you play, it’s unique.
  • Relaxing - Gentle music, or listen to your own.
  • Completely free - No ads, no payment, no sign-up.
  • Play everywhere - From small phones to large 4K screens.

How to play

  • Tap & draw with your finger.
  • Change options in the top-right using the hamburger icon.
  • You can take video recordings or screenshots to use as wallpaper.
  • On PC and Mac, use the keyboard (space to pause). For the full controls guide see the keyboard guide on Steam/Itch.
  • If you like the app, there is a donation option (on mobile devices) in the top-left.
