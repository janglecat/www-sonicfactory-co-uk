Privacy

Sonicfactory & Kinetic Canvas data privacy

  • Sonicfactory and Kinetic Canvas do not see, store or access any of your personal information (PII, aka “personally identifiable information”) for the purpose of using any products created by Sonicfactory.
  • When you purchase a “supporter” unlock key in-application, via Google Play, Apple App Store, or any other app store, this is simply a unique key that enables you to play Kinetic Canvas without interruptions. This key contains no personal data, nor any financial information as used to process the payment via a web store. It simply confirms that your order completed and you “own” the supporter status.
  • No other data is captured or transmitted when you are using the application, other than to validate your supporter status. Again, all this data is completely anonymous.
  • If you have any concerns, please get in contact via the Sonicfactory Discord server.

These cookies are controlled by YouTube and may be used for analytics, personalization, or advertising purposes, even if you do not interact with the video.
  • By using this site, you acknowledge that third-party content providers like YouTube may collect data in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.
